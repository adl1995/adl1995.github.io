Hi, I’m Adeel!

Fri 29 January 2021

I’m a software engineer at CERN in Geneva.

I’ve always been passionate about computers, especially Linux. My setup includes Xmonad on top on Arch Linux with a few handy configs to make life simpler. My evenings are mostly spent pursuing my masters in CS from Georgia Tech. I’m an avid reader of Hacker News.
I love hard technical problems. I’ve worked in Computer Vision, Machine Learning, Robotics, Cyber-security and now trying to get involved in Quantum Computing. I’m also greatly fond of Donald Knuth.

I’m an avid kayaker around Lake Geneva. I live close to a mountain in countryside France, so my weekends are spent hiking around the Alpes. I’m also into photography.
